Project 2

JPS Bakery Website

This project is a website for JPS Bakery, a beloved local bakery known for its delicious assortment of bread, pastries, and cakes. The website serves as an online storefront for JPS Bakery, allowing customers to explore their mouthwatering offerings and conveniently place orders from the comfort of their homes. With a warm and inviting design, the website reflects the bakery's commitment to quality, craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction.

Key features of the website include:

  • Interactive menu showcasing bakery products with mouthwatering images
  • Online ordering system with customizable options for cakes and pastries
  • Special promotions and discounts for loyal customers
  • Information about JP's Bakery's commitment to using locally-sourced ingredients
  • Customer support section for inquiries, feedback, and testimonials
